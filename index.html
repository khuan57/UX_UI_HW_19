About Me

Hey! I'm Kristi, a UX/UI designer based in Atlanta, Georgia.

I recently completed my certification in UX/UI design at Georgia Tech, just after graduated from Emory University with a Bachelors in Business Administration.

Currently I am a marketing coordinator in Atlanta and have worked hands-on with projects that are focused on solving user problems.

I started my passion for UX/UI design with the creation of my baking blog, BaoBeiBaked. Combining my hobby of baking with the creativity and management needed for website and content curation has allowed me to tackle challenges eagerly and develop critical skills.

I love to try new foods at new cities in the world as I try to travel across the globe as much as I can.
